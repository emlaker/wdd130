Rafting

We do a range of different rivers and difficulty levels. We do anything from floating down the river casully to pure adrenaline pumping white water rafting! It is important to understand the differences between the dificulty levels and which one you feel most comfortable with. If you have questions about where you should start or what level is right for you feel free to contact us on any of our social media sites or reach out to our number listed on the site!

Our Crew

Meet our five-star crew! They are rafting pros and certified saftey specialists. They are here to help you in your rafting adventures. Whether it's your first time or you're a seasoned pro these people will be there to guide and support you. They are very friendly people and they looking forward to joining you on your adventure!

Dangers

Although rafting is really fun and full of adventure it is important to realize that there are some risks involved. In the areas where we raft there are certain wildlife that are not so very friendly, like the rattler shown on the left. Spiders and snakes in these desert regions can be very deadly and you should exercise caution and good judgement. There is also risks of being capsized while rafting, especially on the harder rivers. As was stated earlier, our crew are saftey certified and following their guide should keep you safe from harm.
